Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a roof installation typically take?

Most residential roof installations take 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof, weather conditions, and the materials being used. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all roofing projects. Our estimates are detailed and include all materials, labor, and timeline information.

What roofing materials do you work with?

We specialize in asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, slate, and flat roof systems. We'll help you choose the best material for your budget, climate, and aesthetic preferences.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies. We can help document damage, provide estimates, and work directly with your insurance adjuster to ensure proper coverage.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer manufacturer warranties on materials (typically 20-50 years) and our own workmanship warranty (typically 10-25 years). All warranty details are provided in writing before work begins.

How do I know if I need a roof repair or replacement?

Our free inspection service can determine whether repair or replacement is needed. Factors include roof age, extent of damage, and overall condition. We'll provide honest recommendations based on what's best for your home.
